Hi, I'm James!

Librarian, Hobbist Web Developer, and Cat Lover


I am an early career librarian and the current Assistant Director & Morgantown Public Library manger at the Morgantown Public Library. Originally from East Tennessee, I earned my Bachelor's in History and a Master's in Information Sciences from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville in 2020 and 2022. I continue to learn new skills in my free time including web programming, computer hardware, and others. More often than not though, you will find me playing a video game or playing with my cats. I currently live in West Virginia with my fiancee and two cats.

Experience

I am currently the Assistant Director & Morgantown Public Library Manager at MPLS. I am responsible primarily for managing daily operations of the Morgantown Public Library.

From Sept. 21 - Aug. 22, I was the Access Services Student Supervisor and Multimedia Associate at WVU's Downtown Campus Library. I was responsible for managing the student employment program and developing the DCL multimedia collection.

I worked as a library assistant at MCPLS from March to August 2021. I answered patron questions, completed special projects, and helped maintain the library collection.

I volunteered with the Voices Out Loud LGBTQ+ History Project from 2018 to 2020 to help digitize the project's physical collection, process and create metadata records, and make it accessible though its digital archive.

I volunteered as an English as a Second Language instructor at Centro Hispano de East Tennessee from 2019 to 2020 where I taught basic English classes.
